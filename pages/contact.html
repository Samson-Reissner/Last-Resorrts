Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most common repairs like screen replacements are completed within 2-4 hours. Complex motherboard repairs may take 1-2 days.

Do you offer warranty on repairs?

Yes! All our repairs come with a 90-day warranty on labor and 30-day warranty on parts.

Can I get a repair quote online?

Absolutely! Send us photos of your device and description of the issue via email for a free quote.

Do you repair all device brands?

We repair most major brands including Apple, Samsung, Huawei, Dell, HP, Lenovo, and more.
