Who We Are

Your Trusted Electronics Partner in Malawi

From Passion to Profession

Founded in 2018 by a team of passionate tech enthusiasts, Last Resorts began as a small repair shop with a big vision: to provide reliable, affordable electronics solutions for everyone in Malawi.

Frustrated by seeing people overpay for subpar repairs or being told their devices were "beyond repair," we set out to change the electronics service industry with transparency, expertise, and genuine care.


Malawi's Most Trusted Repair Center

As one of the few repair centers in the region with certified technicians across multiple device brands, we're proud to set the standard for quality electronics repair and customer service.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become Malawi's most trusted electronics repair and solutions partner, making technology accessible, affordable, and sustainable for every Malawian.

  • Center for electronics education and skill development

To provide reliable, affordable electronics repair and quality products while educating our community about technology maintenance and sustainability.

  • Exceptional repair services with warranty
  • Quality electronics at fair prices
