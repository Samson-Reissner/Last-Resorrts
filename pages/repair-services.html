Repair FAQs

How long does a typical repair take?

Most common repairs like screen replacements take 1-2 hours. Complex repairs may take 1-2 days. We'll provide an accurate timeline during diagnostics.

Do I need to backup my data before repair?

We always recommend backing up your data. While we take every precaution, some repairs may require data wiping. We can assist with backup services.

What if my device can't be repaired?

If repair isn't cost-effective, we'll provide honest advice and may offer trade-in options toward a new device.

Do you offer on-site repairs?

Currently, all repairs are done at our workshop where we have all necessary equipment for quality work.
